    - Problem: WorkoutPage has `useEffect` that redirects to `/` when `workout.completedAt` is set. But after `finishWorkout()`, completedAt is set, and useLiveQuery will re-render WorkoutPage, potentially triggering the redirect before ActiveWorkout can show the summary.

So keep the redirect but with a small delay or check:
      - Use the simplest approach that works. The key constraint is: summary must be visible after finishWorkout is called.
